Signal Processing

Enhancing space communications by distinguishing meaningful signals from noise.

Overview

Signal Analysis: This system focuses on collecting and analyzing data signals from space missions, creating comprehensive reports, and detecting anomalies to improve communication reliability.

Noise Reduction: Utilizes advanced algorithms to filter out unwanted noise from signal data, ensuring clarity and accuracy in transmissions.
