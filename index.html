Welcome to Signal Processing!

Learn how we separate the main signals from noise and filter modules effectively for space communications.

What are Signals?

Signals are patterns of data that carry information. In space missions, signals are transmitted between spacecraft and Earth, often with added noise.

Module Filtering

Module filtering is a method to extract useful signals and suppress irrelevant data or noise. Key methods include:

  • Low-Pass Filtering
  • High-Pass Filtering
  • Band-Pass Filtering
  • Notch Filtering

Noise Reduction Techniques

Reducing noise is critical to improve the quality of signals. Common techniques include:

  • Fourier Transform
  • Kalman Filtering
  • Wavelet Transform
  • Adaptive Filtering
